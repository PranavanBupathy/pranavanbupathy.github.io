    Projects

    Analysis of digital number from UAV acquired images with respect to altitude, orientation, sensor angle, and weather condition

    This study centered on examining whether factors such as orientation, altitude, sensor angle, and weather conditions influence the quality of images obtained by Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs).

    EDA on ADS-B Flight tracking Data

    The Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast (ADS-B) system is integrated into aircraft transponders. Approximately 70% of commercial aircraft are currently outfitted with this technology. We aim to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on flight profiles using the data derived from these ADS-B systems.
